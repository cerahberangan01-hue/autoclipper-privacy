🔐 Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1. What We Process

AutoClipper only processes video content that is explicitly submitted by authorized users through our dashboard. We do not scrape, crawl, or collect content from TikTok, YouTube, or any third-party platform without explicit user permission.

2. How We Use Your Content

  • Clipping long-form videos into short-form content (Shorts/Reels/TikToks)
  • Adding transformative edits: auto-captions, dynamic cropping, silence removal, metadata generation
  • Uploading to platforms you explicitly authorize (YouTube, TikTok) via official APIs

3. Data Storage & Retention

  • Video files are processed in-memory or temporary storage and deleted after upload
  • Metadata (titles, captions, hashtags) is stored only to facilitate user review & scheduling
  • No personal data is sold, shared, or used for advertising

4. Compliance & Safety

  • All uploads require human review before posting (configurable)
  • Automated moderation filters NSFW, profanity, and copyright-flagged content
  • Rate limiting & account health tracking prevent spam/bot behavior
  • DMCA takedown requests are honored via automated endpoint

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data or disconnect platform authorization at any time via the dashboard or by contacting us.
